You shouldn't have to travel to another country to be immersed in another language. QuickTalk is a way to immerse yourself right now. QuickTalk works by requiring everyone to teach as well as learn. For every minute you spend speaking in your own language, you earn a minute to speak with someone else in their language. Everyone gets 1 hour of time to learn upon signup, but please teach! Otherwise QuickTalk won't work.
QuickTalk was made by Harold Bracy in an effort to keep his language skills sharp.
